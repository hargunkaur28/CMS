# NGCMS Role Hierarchy & Permissions

This document defines the 5 core roles for the Next-Gen College Management System (NGCMS) and their respective scopes and features.

## 🟠 1. SUPER ADMIN (Platform Owner)
**🎯 Scope**: Universal control (SaaS / Multi-tenant level).
**⚙️ Features**:
- Create and manage multiple college instances.
- Global User Management (Add/Remove Admins).
- System-wide configuration and global analytics.

---

## 🔴 2. ADMIN (College Admin)
**🎯 Scope**: Complete command over a specific college’s operations.
**⚙️ Features**:
- **Admissions**: Full application lifecycle management.
- **Academics**: Course/Subject creation, Timetable generation, Teacher assignment.
- **Monitoring**: Attendance reports, exam publishing, and shortage tracking.
- **Fees**: Fee structure definition and financial tracking.
- **Compliance**: NAAC reports and institutional data exports.

---

## 🔵 3. TEACHER / FACULTY
**🎯 Scope**: Academic data entry and student interaction.
**⚙️ Features**:
- **Attendance**: Daily "Quick Mark" and subject-wise tracking.
- **Exams**: Entry of internal and final marks.
- **LMS**: Uploading study materials and assignments.
- **View-only**: Limited access to student profiles for their assigned batches.

---

## 🟢 4. STUDENT
**🎯 Scope**: Personal academic and administrative dashboard.
**⚙️ Features**:
- **Profile**: View personal/academic details and documents.
- **Academics**: View personal timetable and real-time attendance.
- **Exams**: View published results and download hall tickets.
- **Finance**: Online fee payment and receipt history.
- **Interaction**: Assignment submission and notification center.

---

## 🟡 5. PARENT
**🎯 Scope**: Read-only monitoring of ward's performance.
**⚙️ Features**:
- **Tracking**: Real-time attendance monitoring and result views.
- **Payments**: Track fee status and receive alerts.
- **Comms**: Receive automated WhatsApp/Email notifications for absence or critical updates.
